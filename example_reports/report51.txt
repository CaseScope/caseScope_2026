Investigative Summary
---------------------
Huntress has investigated alerts for this host. Due to the nature of these alerts, the host has been isolated.

Evidence suggests the host has been compromised and on 2025-05-17 at 09:54:23 UTC  a user running with SYSTEM privileges performed credential dumping on both this host "ATN51256" and the host "ATN64140".

Unfortunately both hosts are currently offline, limiting further investigation.

We have deployed mass isolation across the network, to deny the adversary further ingress, and we have the following guidance:

- Audit your firewall, VPN, DHCP, and DNS logs to identify the Adversary's initial access and to pre-event reinfection on their reentry. 
- Consider implementing your Incident Response procedures to fully scope the incident.
- Consider resetting passwords for all potentially impacted accounts.
- Review internal log sources to identify potential unauthorized access.
- Ensure that the Huntress agent is deployed to all desired hosts within the environment.
