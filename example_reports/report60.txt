Gootloader / SocGholish: Malware like Gootloader and SocGholish can download additional malicious tooling, provision remote access to a threat actor, and deploy persistence to stay on the host. Hidden NetSupport: NetSupport is a legitimate remote management tool that is being misused by a threat actor to gain unauthorized access and control of the reported endpoint. Threat actors do this to hide their presence, maintain persistence and further exploit the network.
