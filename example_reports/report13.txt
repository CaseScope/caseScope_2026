Investigative Summary:
----------------------
At 2025-10-29 13:37:34 UTC, Huntress observed user "Pete" executing a malicious RMM installer at `C:\Users\Pete\Downloads\modestparty.exe`. The installer was downloaded from `hxxps://3a62a61d[.]newmodestparty[.]pages[.]dev/`, which originated from a phishing email in the user's Yahoo mail inbox. The execution resulted in the deployment of an unauthorized GoTo Resolver RMM, which then reached out to retrieve a malicious ScreenConnect instance from `hxxps://server.willisterwinpress[.]net/Bin/ScreenConnect.ClientSetup.msi?e=Access&y=Guest`. We did not observe the successful installation of the ScreenConnect instance or any subsequent malicious activities on the host.

Threat Descriptions:
--------------------
Malicious Remote Management Tool: A legitimate remote management tool has been misused by a threat actor to gain unauthorized access and control of the reported endpoint. Threat actors do this to hide their presence, maintain persistence and further exploit the network.
