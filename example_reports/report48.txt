Investigative Summary
---------------------
At 2025-05-30 19:48:22 UTC, Huntress observed user "firechief" executing a malicious process invite.exe. The user interacted with a malicious instance of a "Rogue ScreenConnect" via a Phishing attempt. We have isolated the host to prevent further network callbacks.We are reporting for your awareness.

Threat Descriptions:
-------------------
Rogue ScreenConnect: Huntress has been tracking a number of malicious threat actors convincing users via email into running malicious ScreenConnect (ConnectWise Control) installers that give the threat actor remote access to the host.
