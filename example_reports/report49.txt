---------------------
At 2025-05-29 13:42:05 UTC, Huntress observed user "kmeccariello" executing a malicious process "InvoiceOrder845328940.exe" from "C:\Users\kmeccariello\Downloads\". Evidence suggests that this is a renamed ScreenConnect instance that was downloaded from "hxxps[://]porhebusz[.]de/wp-includes/orders/".
